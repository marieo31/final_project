Retinal Implant

-The system is meant to partially restore useful vision to people who have lost their photoreceptors due to retinal diseases such as retinitis pigmentosa (RP) or age-related macular degeneration (AMD).


-Retinal implants introduce visual information into the retina by electrically stimulating the surviving retinal neurons.

Method for Training a Retinal Implant

-With our model we used machine learning to be able to calibrate an implant using less shapes and repetitions to train.
